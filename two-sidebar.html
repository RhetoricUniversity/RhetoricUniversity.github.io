Academics

Rhetoric University students create and apply knowledge by thinking, doing, and carefully crafting their arguments in a rapidly changing world.

Over six schools on one campus offer students boundless opportunities to pursue their passions and collaborate on solving complex global problems.

Undergraduate and Graduate Studies

Rhetoric University offers great programs ranging from Education to Engineering and Biology to Baking and Underwater Basket weaving to Urban Planning and Political Studies. Here students can find the best education in any chosen field. The astronomical caliber of our programs ensures that students will have a fulfilling college experience and a fruitful career.

In addition to having great programs, Rhetoric University also offers students a wide range of opportunities to experience the world. Students will have the opportunity to learn from some of our many guest lecturers. Some of our most popular guests throughout the years have ranged from Stephen Colbert, to Lucile Vaughan Payne, To the disembodied spirits of T.S Eliot and his Hollow Men. Students will also have the opportunity to explore other cultures in our “study abroad” program. A popular favorite for our students abroad is our original Greek Campus where students will have the opportunity to explore the the hometown of our founder, Aristotle. Students can choose to study abroad anywhere in the world because rhetoric is everywhere.
