Admissions

Rhetoric University students come from all across the U.S. and the world, representing diverse experiences, backgrounds, and cultures.

More than 65 departments and programs offer graduate and professional degrees at Rhetoric University. Admission requirements vary greatly among them.

Admissions Process

At Rhetoric University we believe in a holistic admissions process. Prospective students should be able to craft an argument with strong evidence. Early applications must be submitted no later than October 27th, and all application are due by November 15th. Students with strong connections to rhetoric, such as those who have studied and followed the guidance of the knowledgeable Comp Goddesses. Admitted students will be considered for one of our three honors programs, Chiasmus, Zeugma, or Anadiplosis. Students accepted into these honor programs will be notified before the first semester.

Admissions Information

  • Honors Programs: Chiasmus, Zeugma, and Anadiplosis
  • Rhetoric Connections: 71% of students are made up of CompGoddess former students
  • All applications are due on CompGoddess Nally’s birthday, November 15
  • Early application due date is on CompGoddess Roth’s birthday, October 27
  • Priority admission: Given to students who can bake and make an argument using the evidence of dialogue
