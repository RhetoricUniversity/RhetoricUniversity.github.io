Campus Life

A thriving community of accomplished rhetoricians around the world.

Biking is a popular way to get around campus. Transit options also include a free campus shuttle and car-share. Public Safety provides police and emergency services 24 hours a day.

Student Culture

At Rhetoric University we strive to create a welcoming environment for all of our students. Students wishing to enroll in greek life will have the opportunity to join one of three fraternities(Ethos, Pathos, or Logos). Most of out first and second year students live on campus in one of 5 residence halls(Speaker, Context, Subject, Purpose, and Audience). Any student living in one of our 5 residence halls will have any dietary needs catered to. Students seeking to enroll in an athletic club or college sport will have a large variety of choices. Our collegiate sports programs range from Lacrosse to Rhetorical Hop-Scotch to Multiple Choice Olympics to our Big Ten Flickin’ Chicken Competition. Students with an affinity for rhetorically effective posing or vocal exercise have the opportunity to join the AP Comp Yogis or the Off-key Singing club respectively.
